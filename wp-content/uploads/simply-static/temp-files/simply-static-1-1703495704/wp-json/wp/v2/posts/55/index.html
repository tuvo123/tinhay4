There were quite a few people attending the wedding. The neighbor called his worker over and said: – Hey, let’s go see how many people are attending the wedding over there.
\nPhysicians do leave. He placed a piece of wood on the threshold and sat on the wall waiting for guests to leave the house. They start to leave. Everyone who went out tripped over a log, cursed and continued walking. Only an old woman tripped over a log and immediately turned around and pushed the log aside.<\/p>\n

Uncle workers returned to the owner.<\/p>\n

The owner asked:<\/p>\n

– Are there many people there?<\/p>\n

The worker replied:<\/p>\n

– There’s only one person and it’s an old woman.<\/p>\n

– Why so?<\/p>\n

– Because I left a log on the doorstep, everyone tripped over it, but no one bothered to remove it. Then do the same sheep. But an old woman pushed the log aside so that others wouldn’t trip and fall. The only man to do so.
